The Election Journey

The Election Commission of India points out the dates and phases of the upcoming elections. The Model Code of Conduct takes effect.

Citizens verify their names on electoral rolls and register for new Voter IDs.

Candidates file their nominations along with affidavits declaring their assets and criminal records.

Political parties canvas for votes, abiding by ECI guidelines. Stops 48 hours before polling begins.

Voters cast their ballot using EVMs (Electronic Voting Machines) and VVPAT at polling booths.

EVMs are securely stored in strong rooms until counting day, when results are officially declared.
